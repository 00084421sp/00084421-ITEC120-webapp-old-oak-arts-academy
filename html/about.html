Why choose us?

Old Oak Arts Academy is dedicated to nurturing creativity, discipline and artistic expression through immersive, hands-on education.

Our Mission

Our vision is to cultivate a community of artists who are confident, skilled and ready to make an impact in the world of art.

History

Established in 1995, Old Oak Arts Academy has grown from a small local art school into a leading academy recognized for excellence in creative education. Over the years, our students have gone on to perform, exhibit and innovate on national and international stages. Let's hear some of their experiences.
